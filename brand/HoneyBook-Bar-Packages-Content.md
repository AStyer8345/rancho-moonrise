# PAGE 1: Rancho Moonrise Bar Packages

## LET THE GOOD TIMES POUR!

### WELCOME TO THE RM PARTY

With a wide range of packages and services, we aim to create an effortless client experience built around your budget and favorite beverages. We offer a few kinds of bar experiences at Rancho Moonrise.

- Hourly Open Bar Packages
- Consumption Bar Packages

We will connect with you and your planner to confirm headcount, menu details and bar services to make sure you are set up for success :)

# PAGE 2: Open Bar Packages

## RAISE A GLASS TO AN UNFORGETTABLE NIGHT

### Hourly Open Bar Packages

You pick the drinks you would like to serve and we keep the party going by making sure you never run out!

All packages are based on an open bar model and pricing is based on the product, number of guests attending the event and hours of bar service. All packages include cups, napkins and straws but if you would like to provide customized items for your day, you can do so! Please connect with Rancho Moonrise beforehand to confirm customized items you want to provide. We provide 1 bartender for every 50-75 guests, depending on the package chosen.

### The Campfire Bar Package — $7/person/hour
Full service open bar with beer and wine.

Includes:
- 3 beer options
- 1 seltzer or cider option
- 2 house red wines
- 2 house white wines
- 1 sparkling house wine
- Soft drinks, sparkling water
- Ice, cups, napkins, straws
- Printed bar menu
- Bartender(s) included

Minimum spend of $2,000 required. 20% gratuity added onto the bill. 1 bartender for every 75 guests. Children under 5 drink free.

Full service open bar with beer, wine and liquor.

Includes:

- Ice, cups, napkins, straws

Minimum spend of $2,500 required. 20% gratuity added onto the bill. 1 bartender for every 50 guests. Children under 5 drink free.

Full service open bar with beer, wine and liquor.

# PAGE 3: Consumption Bar

## WELL POURED, WELL CELEBRATED

### Consumption Bar Option

Open a bar tab for your guests! We understand that an unlimited bar is not the best fit for every celebration so we offer the consumption bar option. You pay based on the total amount of drinks consumed. The minimum spend required for consumption bar is $1,000. If you prefer to stop at $1,000, close out your tab and let your guest buy their drinks after that, you can do so! Or if you prefer to have your card open all night, you can do that too. You can cover as much or as little once the minimum is hit as you would like. You will choose the drinks that you would like us to serve on the menu, and we will keep track of what we serve and the cost. The tab is paid on the day of the event and closed out with a 20% gratuity added to the bill. You must hire the bartender(s) for this package at $40 an hour. We require 1 bartender for every 50 guests. We will work with you during planning to answer any questions and make sure the bar is appropriately staffed for what you envision!

### Estimated pricing per drink
- Beer, Cider, Seltzer: $5 - $8
- Wine by the glass: $8 - $10
- Cocktails: $10 - $12
- Non-Alcoholic beverages and mocktails: $3 - $7

### Includes:
- Ice
- Garnishes
- Mixers
- Napkins
- Cups
- Straws

## CHEERS, CURATED

### Combo and Cash Bar

Buy the first few rounds, then let your guests take over the bill.

If you would like your guests to take over the bill, we can make that happen! Choose from either of the first two package options — Open Bar or Consumption Bar, and once the minimums are hit or a number of your choice is reached, your guests can start paying for their drinks. You must hire the bartender(s) once the cash bar starts at $40 an hour. We require 1 bartender for every 50 guests. We will work with you during planning to answer any questions and make sure the bar is ready for what you envision! Cash bar pricing will be similar to consumption bar pricing.
