## 10 Seed Q&As — Ask + Answer from Business Account

**Q1:** Are pets allowed?

**A:**
Yes — pets are welcome! There's a $50 pet fee per stay, one pet per reservation, and a 60 lb weight limit. Keep your pup on a leash on the property and you're good to go. Call us at 737-291-1260 if you have specific questions about your pet.

---

**Q2:** What are your check-in and check-out times?

**A:**
Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. We do our best to accommodate early check-in requests when the property allows — just reach out ahead of your stay. Book at ranchomoonrise.com.

---

**Q3:** How do I find out what events are coming up?

**A:**
Check the events calendar at ranchomoonrise.com/events — it's always current. We also post upcoming events on our Instagram (@rancho_moonrise) and Google profile. Events range from live music nights to yoga mornings, crawfish boils, and themed weekends.

---

**Q4:** How do I book a stay or reserve the venue?

**A:**
For overnight stays (safari tents and cabins), book directly at ranchomoonrise.com — or through Cloudbeds. For weddings, private events, or corporate retreats, schedule a tour first: call 737-291-1260 or use the contact form at ranchomoonrise.com/contact.

---

**Q5:** Is alcohol available at the ranch?

**A:**
Yes — drinks are available at The Lodge: margaritas, beer, wine, and more. Private events have full bar service included. We do not have a public walk-in bar — drink service is for guests staying overnight or attending an event.

---

**Q6:** How much does it cost to stay overnight?

**A:**
Rates vary by accommodation type and date. The best way to check pricing and availability is to go to ranchomoonrise.com and browse open dates — or call us at 737-291-1260. We recommend booking direct for the best rates.

---

**Q7:** Are kids welcome?

**A:**
Absolutely — Rancho Moonrise is family-friendly. Kids love the pool, the fire pits, and the donkeys. Just note that the noise curfew is 9:30 PM property-wide, which keeps things comfortable for everyone. Come make some memories.

---

**Q8:** Where do I park?

**A:**
Free parking is available in the general lot on the property. Please note that vehicles cannot stay parked at your tent or cabin site overnight — move to the main lot after unloading. No charge, no hassle.

---

**Q9:** How do I get there from downtown Austin?

**A:**
We're about 20 minutes east of downtown Austin. Plug 20117 Lockwood Road into Google Maps and it'll take you right to us. Easy highway drive — no long haul required. See you soon.

---

**Q10:** What is your cancellation policy?

**A:**
Cancellation terms are included in your booking confirmation. For questions about a specific reservation, call us directly at 737-291-1260 or email through the contact form at ranchomoonrise.com. We're happy to work with you when life happens.
