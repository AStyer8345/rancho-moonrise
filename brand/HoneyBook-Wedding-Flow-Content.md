## Heading
Weddings at Rancho Moonrise

We are thrilled you have chosen Rancho Moonrise as your wedding venue! We care deeply for this property and hope you will enjoy being here as much as we do. Please pass this document on to your Event Coordinator, as it will help answer questions and provide guidelines for vendors to make your event run as smoothly as possible.

## Event Checklist
- Submit signed contract to Rancho Moonrise
- 50% Deposit Paid to Rancho Moonrise
- Damage Deposit paid to Rancho Moonrise
- Event Coordinator information submitted to Rancho Moonrise
- Planner and Vendor Guidelines given to your Event Coordinator
- Rain plan secured
- Submit Catering information and proof of insurance to Rancho Moonrise
- Purchase wedding insurance and submit proof of policy to Rancho Moonrise
- Event Coordinator to provide event timeline and layout to Rancho Moonrise at least three weeks prior to wedding date
- Event Coordinator to provide final questions to Rancho Moonrise at least 1 month prior to the wedding date

**PLEASE PASS ALONG INFORMATION TO ALL VENDORS AS DIFFERENT ITEMS ARE HELPFUL FOR DIFFERENT FOLKS. ALSO PLEASE CLEARLY COMMUNICATE LOAD-IN AND LOAD-OUT TIMES TO ALL VENDORS.**

## INFORMATION FOR EVENT COORDINATORS

We look forward to working with you at Rancho Moonrise! Please note this is general information and requirements learned from experience. We learn new things about our space, event logistics, and vendor relations after each event. These are tips compiled to make your job easier. If something is not clear, always ask! We are here to help. In general, we ask that all vendors respect the property. All the different areas and landscaping are very important to us and they are a huge part of why people choose to host events here. If we feel they are being mistreated, expect to hear from us!

### Event Coordinators
We love event coordinators. You are the folks who make things run smoothly and we greatly appreciate your time and organization. Please always ask for things that might help your event run better and we will make every effort to accommodate if possible. Unless otherwise agreed to in writing, we do require that you or a representative from your company be present at all times while vendors are on the property. We ask that timelines, layouts, our questionnaire and any other pertinent information be submitted at one month in advance. This helps us prepare for your event and troubleshoot any items well in advance of the event.

### Vendor Meetings
Included in the Client contract is a one-hour walk through vendor meeting. Please schedule this in advance as it helps tremendously to nail down details. Due to our heavy event and tour schedule, it is difficult for us to accommodate individual tours for vendors.

### Tenting
All tents and tent vendors must be approved by Rancho Moonrise or secured through one of our preferred vendors. Please contact Rancho Moonrise for that information.

### Rental Inventory
We have a variety of items to rent for your event. [View inventory HERE](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1btwZNG6YPiqkxfUQaan9l5wlG91JHhq1/view)

### Rental Delivery and Pick-up
All vendors must deliver, set-up, breakdown, and exit during the contracted window unless other arrangements are made in writing prior to the event date. Early delivery or late pick-up is based on availability and additional fees apply. Please inquire with management. PLEASE NOTE: Nothing food related (dishware, flatware, napkins, linens, etc.) can stay onsite overnight for late pickup.

### Shuttles
If your client chooses to provide shuttles, the 30-person vehicles work best when pulling down the driveway for drop off. Please confirm your shuttle size with Rancho Moonrise.

### Parking
It will be your responsibility to make sure that vendors park in an organized manner and leave appropriate room for shuttles needing to park for the duration of the event. Vendors CANNOT leave their cars parked by the lodge or barns. They may use these areas for loading and unloading only and then will need to move their vehicles to the designated vendor parking area to the right of the driveway when you pull in.

### Smoking
We are a non-smoking venue. You will be charged for butts not properly disposed of that we have to pick up off the ground after the event.

### Overtime Hours
If you find you will need additional hours or even days for set-up or breakdown, please confirm with Rancho Moonrise prior to your walkthrough. If Client's event or vendors exceed the contracted time, either at the actual event, or in advance set-up time, Client will be charged $500 per hour. If Client purchases additional set-up time in advance and vendors remain on the property past contracted time, Client will be charged for the additional hours separately. It is the Client's responsibility to clearly communicate contracted event times to all vendors. Any last minute hours or time spent monitoring vendors who are on property before or after contracted times will be charged to the Client at $500 per hour.

### Fireworks and Open Flames
Rancho Moonrise does not allow fireworks at any event. Sparklers are permitted in certain areas based on prior approval in writing from Rancho Moonrise.

### Loading and Unloading
Please remind vendors who will be loading and unloading to please be mindful of roads, trees, culverts, and landscaping. Any damaged property in loading and unloading will be charged to the Client.

### Power
It is imperative to anticipate power needs of each vendor well in advance of the event. It is the Client's responsibility to learn all power requirements ahead of time to dictate if a generator is needed.

### Children
Due to the pool and rugged landscapes, there are absolutely NO unattended children permitted at any event. If unattended children are found, they will be escorted back to their guardians by security or staff. If they are found unattended a second time, the guests will be asked to leave. THERE WILL BE NO LIFEGUARD ON DUTY AND THE SAFETY AND CARE OF ANY CHILD USING THE POOL SHALL BE THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE CLIENT AND/OR THE CHILD'S PARENTS. Depending on the number and ages of children attending, additional security or childcare may be required.

### Pets
There is a $50 per pet fee. No pets allowed in the pool area, Lodge or Event Barn.

### DIY
While we fully support creative brides and their families in contributing to décor of the wedding, we require all DIY projects be disassembled and removed during the designated breakdown hours after the wedding. That includes removing all trash and debris used in projects. If a guest is not able to remove projects the night of the wedding, planner will need to be prepared to assist if needed. If removal happens outside of contracted hours, Client will be charged accordingly.

### Decor
No decor is allowed to be taped, nailed or attached to any physical part of all structures at Rancho Moonrise. Attaching decor to any area of the property will result in the Client getting billed and the decor asked to be taken down immediately. This is incredibly important to the Corral area that is a historical piece on the property. Do not touch the wood. If you need to physically attach decor to any part of the property, please consult with Rancho Moonrise management and get approval in writing.

### Intoxicated Guests
We will make every effort possible to keep guests from driving off the property intoxicated. If we need to call a cab or another form of transportation, please notify Rancho Moonrise Management. If guests get a safe ride home, they are more than welcome to leave vehicles overnight and retrieve them the morning following the event from 8am – 9am.

### Insurance
We require Client's to purchase event insurance for a minimum of $1,000,000, and provide a certificate of insurance to Rancho Moonrise at least 1 month (30 days) prior to the date of the event with Rancho Casa Amigos, LLC DBA Rancho Moonrise as an additional insured. We recommend using [wedsure.com](https://www.wedsure.com/).

### Alcohol
We do not allow bridal parties to consume their own alcohol while getting ready. All alcohol must be purchased through Rancho Moonrise to comply with our liability insurance. We have alcohol packages that can be purchased ahead of time, and we will have the cold drinks ready for you and your guests upon arrival.

## CATERING AND BAR SERVICE GUIDELINES
**PLEASE NOTE, THESE GUIDELINES APPLY TO FOOD TRUCKS AS WELL. IF A FOOD TRUCK DOES NOT PROVIDE THESE SERVICES, PLANNER WILL NEED TO ACCOUNT FOR CLEANUP, BUSSING, DRINKING WATER, ETC.**

- Any caterer not on our preferred vendor list must be approved in writing by Rancho Moonrise Management.
- Caterer must provide proof of insurance to Client for Rancho Moonrise.
- Caterers must provide all drinking water needed at an event. If drinking water is not provided, caterer will be asked to go to a nearby store and purchase what is needed.
- Caterers are responsible for removing all trash, food, debris, recycling, and equipment from event space. Debris includes, but is not limited to crumbs, broken glass, cigarette butts, zip ties, safety pins, straight pins, greenery, wax, etc.
- No food related items are allowed to stay onsite overnight for late pickup.
- If using a third-party company for rentals, please clearly communicate hours and location for delivery and pick-up. Any vendor onsite after contracted hours will be billed to the Client at $500/hour. In addition, any late pick-ups need to be approved in advance and in writing by Rancho Moonrise.
- All areas used by caterer must be swept prior to departure.
- Only ice and water may be disposed of on the ground surrounding catering set ups.
- If there are questions about what can or cannot be disposed of, please ask Rancho Moonrise Management.
- All alcohol must be served by TABC certified bartenders through Rancho Moonrise.

Rancho Moonrise will have warm breakfast tacos and coffee waiting for your guests as they wake up the morning after your wedding.

### Package includes:

- Tacos of your choice served with red and green salsa:
  - Chorizo, ham, bacon or sausage with egg
  - Potato, egg and cheese
  - Veggie and egg
  - Bean and potato
  - Flour or gf corn tortillas available
