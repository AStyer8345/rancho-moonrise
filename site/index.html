About the Ranch

20 Acres of Texas Magic

Rancho Moonrise is Austin's first full-service glamping and events property — a working ranch where luxury meets the land. Located in Manor, TX, just 20 minutes from downtown Austin, we offer overnight stays in safari tents and hand-crafted cabins, each with A/C, heat, and private fire pits.

Whether you're planning a destination wedding, a corporate retreat, or a weekend escape, the ranch is yours to explore — from our Event Barn and Neon Moon Bar & Lounge to the poolside deck and oak-shaded pastures with sweeping Hill Country views.

Family-friendly. Pet-friendly. Unforgettable.
