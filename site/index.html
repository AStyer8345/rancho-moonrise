About the Ranch

20 Acres of Texas Magic

Rancho Moonrise is Austin's first full-service glamping and events property — located in Manor, TX, just 20 minutes from downtown Austin. The property spans 20 acres with safari tents, cabins, a resort-style pool, the Event Barn, and the Neon Moon Bar & Lounge.

Whether you're planning a destination wedding, a corporate retreat, or a weekend escape with friends, the ranch is yours to explore.

Glamping Near Austin, TX

The Best Glamping Near Austin, Texas

Rancho Moonrise is Austin's premier glamping destination — located in Manor, TX, just 20 minutes from downtown Austin. The property spans 20 acres and offers 20 units including luxury safari tents and hand-crafted cabins, each with air conditioning, heating, and private fire pits. Unlike traditional camping, every accommodation comes fully furnished with a comfortable bed, mini fridge, and toiletries included.

Guests can access the resort-style pool, General Store, and Neon Moon Bar & Lounge during their stay. The ranch is family-friendly, pet-friendly (one dog up to 60 lbs, $50 fee), and checks in between 4–8 PM daily. Reservations book directly through Cloudbeds with real-time availability.

Rancho Moonrise also hosts live music events, yoga sessions, crawfish boils, and destination weddings — making it a full weekend destination rather than just a place to sleep. Weekend dates fill quickly, especially during Austin's spring and fall seasons.
