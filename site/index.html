About the Ranch

36 Acres of Texas Magic

Rancho Moonrise is Austin's glamping and events ranch — 20 minutes from downtown Austin. Safari tents and hand-crafted cabins, a resort-style pool, the Event Barn, The Lodge, and plenty of room to roam. Donkeys at the fence line and ranch life all around.

Whether you're planning a destination wedding, a corporate retreat, or a weekend escape with friends, the ranch is yours to explore.
