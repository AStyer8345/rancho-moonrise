Issue 2 — Critical

Missing from the #1 Glamping Discovery Platform

Glamping Hub is where travelers specifically search for glamping — safari tents, cabins, unique stays. Your competitors Talula Mesa and Walden Retreats are listed. Rancho Moonrise is completely absent. This is the single most targeted audience for your overnight business, and they can't find you.

High-intent glamping travelers are booking elsewhere
Fix: I'll create your Glamping Hub listing with optimized descriptions and your best photos. Also setting you up on Tentrr and Outdoorsy.
Issue 3 — Critical

Your Google Maps Listing Is Incomplete

When someone searches "glamping near me" or "event venue Austin," Google shows a map with the top 3 businesses. Your Google Business Profile is missing a description entirely — the most important text box Google gives you for free. Your closest competitor, Grand Lady Austin (also in Manor), has 86 reviews. You have 8. Google ranks businesses with more reviews higher in that map.

8 reviews vs. competitor's 86 = lower map ranking = fewer clicks
Fix: I'll write your GBP description, add all amenities, upload keyword-named photos, and set up a simple review request system so you start collecting reviews after every stay and event.
Issue 4 — Critical

AI Assistants Don't Know You Exist

300 million people use ChatGPT every week. Many ask "where's a good glamping spot near Austin?" or "outdoor wedding venue Austin TX." I tested this — ChatGPT and Perplexity recommend Lucky Arrow Retreat and Sinya on Lone Man Creek. Rancho Moonrise doesn't come up. This is a massive, invisible source of lost bookings that grows every month.

Every AI recommendation = a booking that goes to a competitor
Fix: The new website's schema markup is the foundation. I'll also add Rancho Moonrise to Foursquare (ChatGPT's main business database) and Yelp. As press coverage and directory listings grow, AI tools start recommending you.
