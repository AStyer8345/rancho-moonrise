Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to Rancho Moonrise.

  • Maximum 1 dog per reservation

All accommodations include A/C, heating, lighting, electrical outlets, an outdoor fire pit, and complimentary soap, shampoo, and conditioner. Safari tents also include a mini-refrigerator. Cabins have a cooler.

Tents #13–#20: Private ensuite bathrooms.

Tents #7–#12: Shared communal bathhouse (clean, well-maintained, and a short walk from your tent).

Yes — a resort-style pool and spa available to all overnight guests. Pool closes at 9 PM. Towels provided at the front desk. No lifeguard on duty.

Day pool passes are also available for non-guests via ResortPass.

No outside alcohol is allowed at Rancho Moonrise. Beer, wine, snacks, and essentials are available at the on-site General Store.

Absolutely. The ranch is kid and family friendly with play areas and a pool. Pack-and-plays fit in safari tents (not cabins). Safari tents #7–#8 are recommended for families with young children.

One complimentary parking spot per reservation in the general lot. Vehicles cannot remain at individual sites overnight. Golf cart service is available to shuttle you and your luggage to your site.

Complimentary WiFi is available in the Lodge. The ranch is designed for unplugging — but we understand you may need to check in.

Yes — every site has a private fire pit. Fires must be extinguished by midnight. Fire pit use is subject to any active county fire bans. Firewood is available for purchase at the General Store.

Quiet hours begin at 9:30 PM. This is a shared property with families and other guests — please be respectful of noise levels after curfew.

Full refund if canceled within 5 days of booking. No refunds for inclement weather. Please review our full policies page for details.
