What to Expect

What Is Glamping Near Austin, Texas?

Glamping — short for glamorous camping — combines outdoor sleeping with the comfort of a hotel. At Rancho Moonrise, that means air-conditioned safari tents with real beds, hand-crafted cabins, a resort pool, and fire pits throughout the property — all on 36 acres of open Texas ranch land, 20 minutes from downtown Austin.

Our safari tents range from shared-bathhouse configurations to fully private ensuite options. Cabins also use the shared communal bathhouse. Every unit comes with A/C, heat, linens, soap, shampoo, and conditioner — no gear required. Just show up.

Check-in is 3–5 PM daily. Check-out is 11 AM. Pets are welcome. Firewood is available for purchase. The Lodge carries beer, wine, and snacks so you don't have to leave the ranch. Weekend dates — especially those coinciding with live music events — fill weeks in advance.
