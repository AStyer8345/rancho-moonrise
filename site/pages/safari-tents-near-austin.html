What Are Safari Tents Near Austin, Texas?

Safari tents near Austin are glamping-style canvas accommodations that combine outdoor sleeping with hotel-level comfort. At Rancho Moonrise — located 20 minutes east of downtown Austin on 36 acres — safari tents feature real queen or king beds, air conditioning, heat, and mini fridges, with resort pool access and fire pits throughout the property. No gear, no sleeping bags, no setup. Just show up.

Rancho Moonrise is the closest full-service safari tent destination to Austin. The property offers multiple safari tent configurations — single-bed tents, family-size tents, and premium ensuite options with private bathrooms — alongside hand-crafted cabins. Every stay includes resort pool access, The Lodge on-site, and the full 36-acre ranch.
