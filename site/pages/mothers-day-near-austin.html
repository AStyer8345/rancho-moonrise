The Yoga & Mimosas Morning — Best for the Active Mom

Rancho Moonrise runs a monthly Yoga & Bottomless Mimosas event on Sunday mornings — vinyasa yoga poolside followed by unlimited mimosas. Mother's Day 2026 falls on a Sunday (May 11), which aligns with this regular series.

It's a 2-hour morning: class starts at 10:30 AM, mimosas flow after. You don't need to be an experienced yogi. The vibe is casual, the setting is a 36-acre working ranch, and the bottomless mimosas are a reasonable substitute for the brunch you were going to skip anyway.

What to Bring

Whether you're coming for a pool day pass or a full overnight, the essentials are the same:

  • Sunscreen — May sun near Austin is real. The pool area has some shade but you'll want SPF.
  • Swimsuit and cover-up — pool is the anchor activity
  • Cash or card for The Lodge — drinks, snacks, and essentials on property
  • Light layers for evening — May evenings cool down. A fire pit at dusk is worth staying for.
  • Comfortable shoes — the ranch is 36 acres. You'll walk on grass and gravel.

Overnight guests: check-in details and packing recommendations are at the FAQs page. The ranch provides linens, towels, and basic toiletries — you don't need to haul camping gear.
