A Private Event Venue Near Austin Worth Talking About

Rancho Moonrise is a private event venue on 36 acres, 20 minutes east of downtown Austin. The property includes a climate-controlled Event Barn (up to 200 guests), a resort-style pool, and open pastures under the Texas sky. Available for hourly rentals or full ranch buyouts, with overnight accommodations for up to 50 guests on-site.

What makes Rancho Moonrise different from a typical event venue near Austin: your guests never share the property with strangers. Whether you're hosting a corporate off-site for 30 or a private festival for 200, the entire ranch is yours for the day — or the weekend.
