Frequently Asked Questions

Rancho Moonrise is a full-service ranch wedding venue 20 minutes from downtown Austin. The property offers exclusive full-ranch buyouts for weddings, meaning no outside guests will be on the property during your event. With unlimited ceremony layouts across 36 acres, an indoor Event Barn, a pool, and capacity for 200+ guests, it's one of the few venues near Austin that handles ceremony, reception, and overnight stays all in one location.

The combination of exclusivity and on-site overnight accommodations sets Rancho Moonrise apart from most Austin-area wedding venues. When you book a wedding here, you're not sharing the property with other events or day guests — the entire 36-acre ranch is yours. Your wedding party can sleep on-site in hand-crafted cabins and safari tents, eliminating the hotel block coordination problem that trips up most wedding planners. Austin has dozens of wedding venues, but very few offer this level of full-property exclusivity with overnight stays built in.

Rancho Moonrise accommodates up to 200+ guests for ceremonies and receptions, with on-site sleeping accommodations for up to 50 overnight guests across cabins and safari tents.
