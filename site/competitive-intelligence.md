## Section 2 — Competitor Highlights

### New Entrants This Week

#### The Yurtopian — [theyurtopian.com](https://theyurtopian.com)
**Location:** Two Hill Country locations — Dripping Springs (10 yurts, 46 acres, 35 min from Austin) + Wimberley (6 yurts, ~30 min from Austin)  
**What they have:** 16 luxury Mongolian-style yurts total. Per-yurt amenities: king bed, climate control, private hot tub or plunge pool, fire pit, rooftop viewing deck with hammock, outdoor shower, kitchenette, WiFi. On-site spa massage. Catered picnic experiences.  
**Events/corporate:** Yes — corporate retreats, elopements, and group events.  
**Why it matters:** Now ranking in "glamping near austin tx." Scale (16 units) and Hill Country proximity (35 min) make this a direct competitor. Their Dripping Springs location is the same corridor as Lucky Arrow. Represents the 6th individually-ranked glamping competitor since Rancho Moonrise's April 9 baseline.

#### Spoon Mountain Glamping — [spoonmountainglamping.com](https://spoonmountainglamping.com)
**Location:** Wimberley, TX (~50-60 min from Austin)  
**What they have:** Safari tents imported from South Africa with private pools, luxury vinyl plank floors, soaking tubs under the stars, custom light fixtures. Adults-only positioning.  
**Why it matters:** Now appearing in "safari tent austin texas" SERP alongside Safari for the Soul. The safari tent keyword is now contested by 3+ properties. Combined with Safari for the Soul's brand-keyword dominance and Lucky Arrow's 3 in-house safari tent units, the "safari tent" differentiator Rancho Moonrise launched a landing page to own is increasingly crowded.
