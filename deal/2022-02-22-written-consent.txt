UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF MANAGERS

IN LIEU OF

ORGANIZATIONAL MEETING

OF

RANCHO CASA AMIGOS, LLC

The undersigned, being all of the Managers of Rancho Casa Amigos, LLC, a Texas limited liability company (the "Company"), hereby consent in writing to the taking of the following action and to the adoption of the following resolutions:

RESOLVED, that the Certificate of Formation of the Company, as filed with the Secretary of State of the State of Texas on April 12, 2021, is hereby in all respects confirmed, ratified, approved and adopted; and that upon election, the Secretary of the Company is hereby directed to insert such Certificate of Formation, as certified by the Secretary of State of the State of Texas, in the minute book of the Company; and be it further

RESOLVED, that the Company Agreement of the Company, dated as of April 12, 2021, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit A (the "Company Agreement"), is hereby approved, and each officer of the Company, or any of them be, and they hereby are, in all respects authorized, empowered and directed, for and on behalf of the Company to execute the Company Agreement on behalf of the Company; and be it further
